pGain GLZMath

pGain : Remapping the unit interval into the unit interval by expanding the sides and compressing the center, and keeping 1/2 mapped to 1/2
This was a common function in RSL tutorials (the Renderman Shading Language). (K :k=1 is the identity curve, k<1 produces the classic gain() shape, and k>1 produces "s" shaped curces.
Note : The curves are symmetric (and inverse) for k=a and k=1/a.
